Hell's Angels

Outlaw motorcycle gang responsible for Altamont security for the Rolling Stones. Gonzo journalist Hunter S. Thompson wrote a groundbreaking, definitive work on the group.

Timeline

1967 Hunter S. Thompson writes Hell's Angels: A Strange and Terrible Saga.
3 Dec 2003 ATF and other authorities raid Hell's Angels facilities as part of a two-year undercover operation, encompassing 35 states. Seven hundred officers serve eighty search warrants, and 57 individuals arrested. Authorities did not say what they were looking for, but it is believed to be illicit firearms and information or material related to the Hell's Angels production and distribution of methamphetamine.
