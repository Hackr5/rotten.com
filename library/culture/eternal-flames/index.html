Eternal Flames

TO ELVIS IN MEMORIUM

You gave yourself to each of us in
some manner. You were wrapped in
thoughtfulness and tied with love.
May this flame reflect our never
ending respect and love for you.
May it serve as a constant reminder
to each of us of your eternal presence.

We all went to Graceland and went to Elvis's grave and next to the grave there was a plastic box with rainwater in it. I asked a security guard what it was and he said it "was the eternal flame." We mentioned that it wasn't burning, and he said "It's a semi-eternal flame." The spirit of the song was inspired there. Blame it on Elvis.
-- Michael Steele of the Bangles

timeline

Oct 1997 French police arrest an Australian man for using the eternal flame at the Arc de Triomphe in Paris to cook an egg.
2 Jul 1998 The eternal flame at the Arc de Triomphe in Paris, which has burned continuously since 1921 to commemorate the dead of World War I, is extinguished by an intoxicated soccer fan with his beer-enriched urine. Mexican national Rodrigo Rafael Ortega is arrested and charged with public drunkenness and offending the dead.
1 Nov 2000 German Chancellor Gerhard Schroder accidentally extinguishes the Yad V'Shem eternal flame during a ceremony at the Holocaust memorial in Israel.
21 Feb 2001 Local officials extinguish the eternal flame in Troy, Alabama. It had been lit less than a year before, on May 6, 2000.
27 May 2001 An unnamed man attempts to extinguish the eternal flame at the Arc de Triomphe in Paris, by sitting on it. He is promptly arrested and sent to Bichat hospital to treat his burns.
Jan 2002 An unnamed French soldier attempts to urinate on the perpetual flame at the Arc de Triomphe in Paris. He is promptly arrested and sent to a mental clinic.
18 Mar 2004 The Birmingham city council extinguishes their eternal flame dedicated to world peace (lit 31-Dec-1999) after refusing to pay the gas bill.
