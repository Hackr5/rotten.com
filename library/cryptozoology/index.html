Cryptozoology

All Creatures Great and Fabricated

Throughout history, a number of creatures have entered the stuff of legend while themselves being (apparently) non-existent; stories and accounts of the creatures have been passed from generation to generation while proof and evidence have not. Occasionally, a photograph, film or trail will be found that points to the creature being real, but many times these rock-solid proofs crumble like sand in the harsh light of inquiry. They are, in many ways, science's last poetry.
