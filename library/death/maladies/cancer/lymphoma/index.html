Lymphoma

Deaths

26 Aug 1974 Aviator Charles Lindbergh dies of lymphatic infection and cancer, at his home in Hana, Maui HI. He is buried at Kipahulu in a eucalyptus coffin. And the Kipahulu natives don't want your haole ass there.
27 Jul 1980 The Shah of Iran dies of lymphatic cancer in Cairo, Egypt.
22 Sep 1987 Dan Rowan of Rowan and Martin's Laugh-In dies of lymphoma, Englewood FL. Say goodnight, Dick.
20 May 1994 Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis, wife of John F. Kennedy and Aristotle Onassis, dies of non-Hodgkins lymphoma.
27 Aug 1997 Former president of NBC, Brandon Tartikoff dies of Hodgkin's disease at UCLA Medical Center in Los Angeles, CA. Among Tartikoff's achievements at the network is Manimal. He is buried at Mt. Sinai Memorial Park also in Los Angeles.
2 Oct 1998 Country-western signer Gene Autry dies of lymphoma at his home in North Hollywood CA. Estimated net worth, $350M. Buried at Forest Lawn, Hollywood Hills.
7 Feb 1999 King Hussein of Jordan dies from non-Hodgkins lymphoma, at a hospital in Amman Jordan, after six months of unsuccessful treatment at the Mayo Clinic in the United States.
15 Apr 2001 Ramones singer Joey Ramone dies of lymphoma at a New York City hospital. He is buried in Hillside Cemetery, Woodhurst NJ.
15 Mar 2003 Former CEO of Adolph Coors Brewing Co., Joseph Coors dies of lymphatic cancer at his home in Rancho Mirage, CA. Was instrumental in the creation of the Heritage Foundation think tank.
