Kidney Cancer (and urinary tract)

Deaths

13 Jan 1978 Politician Hubert Humphrey dies of bladder cancer, after extensive chemotherapy, Waverly, MN. His description of chemo: "bottled death."
12 Sep 1983 Actor Raymond Burr dies of kidney cancer at his farm near Healdsburg, CA. Buried in Fraser Cemetery, New Westminster, BC, Canada.
26 Aug 1986 Actor Ted Knight (born as Tadeus Wladyslaw Konopka ) dies of urinary tract cancer after battling the disease for nine years, Pacific Palisades, CA. Buried at Forest Lawn, Glendale, CA.
