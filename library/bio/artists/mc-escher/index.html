M.C. Escher

M.C. Escher is the consummate artist for people who don't like art. His mind-bending images have graced the walls of more engineers, mathematicians, physcists and Linux users than the fantabulous imagescapes of Frank Frazetta.*




* Note: This statement only applies to office walls. When bedroom walls are figured into the calculations, Frazetta wins by a wide margin.
