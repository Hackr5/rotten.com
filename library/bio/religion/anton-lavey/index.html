Anton LaVey

aka Howard Stanton Levey

Author of The Satanic Bible, a wacky book mixing LaVey's personal philosophies and life experiences. LaVey rails against the patent absurdity built into modern religions. Then he coughs up a big hunk of fanciful dogma and meaningless ceremonies for his own brand of unspirituality, which he calls Satanism. Complete and utter hypocrite.

Not to mention the fact that LaVey plagiarized Ayn Rand's Atlas Shrugged and other works for his stupid book.
