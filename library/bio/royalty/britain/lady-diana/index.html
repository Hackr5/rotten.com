Lady Diana

Timeline

1 Jul 1961 Lady Diana Francis Spencer born, to 8th Earl Spencer.
29 Jul 1981 Marries Prince Charles.
21 Jun 1982 Son, William Arthur Philip Louis Windsor born after 17 hours of labor, St. Mary's Hospital, Paddington. 7 lbs, 1.5 oz. Armand Hammer is the child's Godfather.
15 Sep 1984 Son, Henry Charles Albert David Windsor born, St. Mary's Hospital, Paddington, 4:20 pm. 6 lbs, 14 oz.
15 Jun 1992 Book, Diana: Her True Story.
25 Aug 1992 James Hewitt tape revealed by the Sun tabloid, revealing an adulterous affair in which Diana's pet name is Squidgy.
20 Nov 1995 In a television interview, admits her adultery with James Hewitt.
28 Aug 1996 Divorce with Prince Charles final.
Oct 1996 According to Paul Burrell, Diana writes a letter in which she states that certain parties are trying to kill her, and will do so using the pretext of an automobile accident. Burrell does not name the party, but says it is in said letter, which he has possession of in a safe place. We can only speculate as to the reasons for her thoughts, but it may involve collecting data, particularly information regarding allegations about Prince Charles that surface in the November 2003 royal scandal.
31 Aug 1997 Diana and boyfriend Dodi Fayed killed in an automobile accident while fleeing from papparazzi, Paris.
6 Sep 1997 Funeral at Westminster Abbey.
Oct 2003 Based on allegations by a Glyn Jones, German magazine Das Neue (#41) states that Diana was killed by MI-5.
