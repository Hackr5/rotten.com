The Krays

Ronnie and Reggie Kray were identical twins and the most notorious, violent criminals in London in the 1960s. They ran protection rackets, entertained celebrities and were finally arrested for murdering other gangsters. In 1969 they were sent to prison with a recommendation that they serve 30 years.
