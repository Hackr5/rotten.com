This "Rebel Without a Clue" commenced a murder spree that was unparalleled for more than three decades. You may have heard of him in such films as Natural Born Killers or Badlands, though perhaps killing was the only thing Starkweather excelled at. His boss: "Sometimes you'd have to tell him something two or three times. Of all the employees in the warehouse, he was the dumbest man we had."
