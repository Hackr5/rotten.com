Moses Sithole

Certainly South Africa's most prolific serial killer, murdered 37 women, and one child. Operated out of three cities: Johannesburg, Pretoria, and Boksburg. Sithole would play games with the local news media, much like the Zodiac Killer in the United States did. Sentenced to 2410 years prison.
