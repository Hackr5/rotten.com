Andrew Cunanan

Andrew Cunanan was a good-looking, highly-intelligent gay murderer whose victims included Gianni Versace, a world-famous fashion designer with a taste for bad taste. Cunanan stalked and shot Versace in July of 1997, pumping two bullets into his brain outside his home. A nationwide manhunt ensued ending in Cunanan's death at his own hand. Cunanan had a high IQ (147) but obviously a loose screw as well.
