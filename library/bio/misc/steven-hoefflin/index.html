Steven M. Hoefflin, M.D., F.A.C.S.

Hollywood plastic surgeon who has (reportedly) worked on:

  • Pamela Anderson
  • Tony Curtis
  • Phyllis Diller's face
  • Zsa Zsa Gabor
  • Janet Jackson
  • Michael Jackson's nose
  • Don Johnson
  • Brigitte Nielsen
  • Dolly Parton
  • Joan Rivers
  • Nancy Sinatra
  • Sylvester Stallone
  • Elizabeth Taylor
  • Ivana Trump

Friends with Heidi Fleiss and Hugh Hefner. His next-door neighbor in Bel-Air is Magic Johnson.

Barbara Maywood, who worked for the surgeon for 17 years, claimed actor Sylvester Stallone marched into an operating theatre where his girlfriend was having a boob job and ordered Dr Hoefflin to make her breast even larger.

Stallone allegedly said he wanted them "big, but like a 17-year-old's".

Model Angie Everhart, who was under general anaesthetic, had agreed on smaller implants, but Maywood said Dr Hoefflin ignored her instructions and did as Stallone asked.

Everhart was said to be so horrified by the result she insisted on another operation three months later restoring her boobs to their original size.
