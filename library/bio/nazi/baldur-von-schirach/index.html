Baldur von Schirach

Timeline

9 Mar 1907 Baldur von Schirach born, Berlin.
1925 Joins the NSDAP.
1926 Meets Adolf Hitler.
1940 Appointed Gauleiter of Vienna.
2 Oct 1940 In Berlin, notifies Hans Frank that there are 50,000 Juden in Vienna for him.
1 Oct 1966 Released, along with munitions minister Albert Speer, from Spandau prison in Berlin after their twenty year sentence. This leaves one solitary prisoner maintained at the facility, Rudolf Hess.
8 Aug 1974 Baldur von Schirach dies.
