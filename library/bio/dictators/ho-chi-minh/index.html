Ho Chi Minh

Ruler of Vietnam. A great admirer of the U.S. Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution, but we spurned him early on, and he turned Marxist after that.

Timeline

19 May 1890 Ho Chi Minh officially born, though other dates are given varying 1890 to 1904.
1911 Leaves Saigon.
1918 In Paris, hangs around with Socialists.
1934 In Moscow until 1938.
2 Sep 1969 Ho Chi Minh dies, Hanoi Vietnam.
