Mobutu Sese Seko

aka Mobutu Sese Seko Kuku wa za Banga

Dictator of Zaire (formerly Congo). Stole billions from his national treasury. Eventually fled to Switzerland.

Full name translates to "The all-powerful warrior who, because of his endurance and inflexible will to win, will go from conquest to conquest leaving fire in his wake."

Made friends with both televangelist Pat Robertson and Nation of Islam leader Louis Farrakhan.
