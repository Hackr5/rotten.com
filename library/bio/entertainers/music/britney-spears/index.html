Britney Spears

Timeline

2 Dec 1981 Britney Jean Spears born, Kentwood, LA.
1990 Contestant on Star Search.
1993 Britney Spears, Christina Aguilera, and Justin Timberlake join the cast of the third iteration of The Mickey Mouse Club.
1996 MMC ends production.
1999 ...Baby One More Time.
1 May 1999 Britney Spears' brand-new boob job premieres on Nickelodeon's Kid's Choice Awards.
2000 Oops! ...I Did It Again.
2001 Britney.
10 Mar 2002 Around 4 AM, Britney Spears is pelted with buckets of urine while filming a television commercial in downtown Los Angeles.
2003 In The Zone.
29 Aug 2003 While performing "Like a Virgin" on the MTV Music Video Awards, shares a flappy open-mouthed kiss with Madonna.
26 Oct 2003 After breaking up with lover Justin Timberlake, Britney Spears tells Newsweek: "There was a time when I was like, 'OK, I'm over men. They're mean.' For like six months, not a single thing happened. Not like they weren't drawn to me, but there wasn't a single real attraction. I'm like, 'What's happening? I know I'm not a lesbian.'"
3 Jan 2004 Marries childhood friend Jason Allen Alexander as a joke, signing annulment papers shortly after the ceremony.
18 Sep 2004 Marries back-up dancer Kevin Federline.
14 Sep 2005 Almost exactly a year after marrying Federline, Spears gives birth to their son, Preston Michael Spears Federline, by caesarean section.
