Jerry Lee Lewis

Cousin of televangelist Jimmy Swaggart. Twice, he has overlapped his marriages. The 1957 marriage to his very young cousin was particularly troublesome in England, where they don't take kindly to that sort of thing as they do in the South. He was booed offstage and cut the tour short.
