Charlton Heston

Will be remembered by posterity primarily as the star of Soylent Green.

Also "Planet of the Apes," "The Ten Commandments," "Touch Of Evil."

Heston was one of the directors of the Nicaraguan Freedom Fund, a nonprofit organization created at the behest of Oliver North to help fund the Contras. $100,000 in seed money was provided by the personal assistant to Sun Myung Moon.

Currently very enthusiastically leading the National Rifle Association.

Has Alzheimer's.
