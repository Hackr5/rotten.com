Charlie Chaplin

Played the Adolf Hitler character in his classic 1940 comedy "The Great Dictator." Prior to this, Hitler was a big fan of Chaplin's. Also played the title character in "Monsieur Verdoux" (the 1947 dark comedy based on the activities of French serial killer Henri Landru).
