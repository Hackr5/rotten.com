Bisexuality

Anything that moves, baby. Anything that moves.

Thanks to Dr. Alfred Kinsey and the sexologists who followed in his juicy footsteps, we know that very few people are exclusively straight or gay. Just about everyone's sexual orientation lies somewhere between 1 and 5 on the Kinsey Scale. But that's kind of like saying most people are ambidextrous -- just because you can masturbate with either hand, it doesn't make you ready to have your good arm torn off by a harvester.

Every straight person has had homosexual dreams, daydreams, and impulses at one time or another. That doesn't make you queer -- it doesn't even make you bi. Until you find yourself actually craving a little suck-and-fuck from somebody with the same sex organs as yours, you're strictly a wannabe.
