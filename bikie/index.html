Bikie Massacre in Australia
rotten.com

On 2 September 1984 two motorcycle "bikie" clubs, the Comanchero's and the Bandido's, escalated their rivalry into open warfare. The place chosen was the "Viking Tavern" in Milperra, a suburb of Sydney, NSW, Australia.
