'It's the end of the world, we're all going to die,' he screamed. ... However, Ms Dugarry added that he had said he spoken about getting rid of some Jehovah's Witnesses who had called on him."

Milosevic finally linked to Srebrenica massacre
Chicago Sun-Times | Submitted by: Tim Howard
"Prosecutors at the war crimes trial of Slobodan Milosevic may have produced the first document linking the former Yugoslav president to the massacre of thousands of Bosnian Muslims in Srebrenica, legal experts said Friday. The killing of 7,500 Muslims at the UN-protected enclave of Srebrenica in July 1995 was the worse massacre in Europe since World War II. Milosevic faces 66 counts of war crimes, including genocide for Srebrenica. He has denied knowledge or involvement in the killings. He has been on trial since February 2002 and is defending himself despite serious heart trouble and illness that has repeatedly delayed hearings.
