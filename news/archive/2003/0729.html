French police using odors to ID perps
London Telegraph | Submitted by: UncleGum
"Scientists working for French police have perfected a technique for 'bottling' smells at the scene of a crime to identify suspects by the odour they have left behind. After conducting a two-year-long programme of tests on a method of detection known as 'odourology,' they have concluded that smell can be as effective as using fingerprints or DNA samples to link a criminal with a crime. Preposterous.] The French national police force's Scientific and Technical Unit, based in Lyon, established that trained sniffer dogs could be presented with a crime's 'smell signature,' then successfully match it to the correct suspect in the olfactory equivalent of an identity parade."
