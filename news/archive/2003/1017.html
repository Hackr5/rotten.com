'The government concedes that the evidence did not establish an intent to "damage" within the meaning of the statute (Computer Fraud and Abuse Act), and requests that this Court reverse the defendant's conviction,' Ronald Cheng, assistant U.S. attorney for the Central District of California, stated in the filing...
