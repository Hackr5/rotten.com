Alleged serial killer formally charged
KOMO-TV 4 Seattle | Submitted by: Reid Fleming
"Robert Pickton, a former pig farmer accused of being Canada's worst serial killer, will stand trial on 15 counts of first-degree murder, a provincial court judge ruled Wednesday. The deaths in which the 53-year-old Pickton is charged are part of a long list of women who disappeared mysteriously over the past 20 years, most from Vancouver's seedy Downtown Eastside. ... Police conducted a massive raid on Feb. 6, 2002, at a farm owned by Pickton and his brother and sister. Pickton was charged with the first two murder counts on Feb. 22, 2002. ... Pickton is charged in the deaths of: Sereena Abotsway, Mona Wilson, Diane Rock, Jacqueline McDonell, Heather Bottomley, Andrea Joesbury, Brenda Wolfe, Jennifer Furminger, Helen Hallmark, Patricia Johnson, Georgina Papin, Heather Chinnock, Tanya Holyk, Sherry Irving and Inga Hall."
