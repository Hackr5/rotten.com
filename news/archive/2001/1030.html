They will know where you were
Guardian | Submitted by: Soylent
It's well known that if you have a cellular phone, your location can be pinpointed fairly accurately. But now it's been revealed that since 1999, Virgin (a cell UK telephone provider) has been keeping records of the locations of the phones -- forever. Which means that they can track your whereabouts over that span of time, who who you visit, where you go, how often you potty, etc. Big Corporate is Watching You.
