Adding Stories
If you are a rotten newshound and you would like to suggest a story, visit our submit page to send us the URL. Stories that are accepted will have your name credited.

Posting Comments
In order to post comments to stories, you will have to create an account. Accounts will generally be validated within 24 hours, and of course will be disabled if there is abuse.

Viewership

New South Wales to secede from Internet
EFF Australia | Submitted by: Soylent
The legislature of the province of New South Wales in Australia will soon decide if it wants to participate in the Internet. Basically, they want to criminalize all content that is "inappropriate for children" -- that is a much lower basis than even "indecent" or "obscene". And furthermore, it treats online publishing very differently than print. It's all crazy, but then again, NSW is to that country what West Virginia is to the USA.
