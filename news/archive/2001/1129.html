Networks sue ReplayTV
Reuters | Submitted by: anonymous
The various networks are scared shitless of digital home recording technology, because it allows a viewer to skip commercials and potentially share content. Most "egregious" of these is ReplayTV, which not only has this feature, but it holds 320 hours of TV and lets you swap with your friends over the Internet. Networks are claming "copyright violation" in their suits, which is nonsense as time shifting of television is protected by law. [Just about everyone on the Rotten Staff has a TiVo, and if you watch any TV at all, these devices are worth every penny.]
