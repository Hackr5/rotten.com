Adding Stories
If you are a rotten newshound and you would like to suggest a story, visit our submit page to send us the URL. Stories that are accepted will have your name credited.

In order to post comments to stories, you will have to create an account. Accounts will generally be validated within 24 hours, and of course will be disabled if there is abuse.

Viewership

National ID cards coming
Washington Post | Submitted by: Justin
Apparently some people continue to believe that a national I.D. card system could have prevented September 11th, never minding that the attackers had legitimate passports, visas, and driver's licenses. Now they're asking Congress for $100 million funding to study an end run, coercing the states into this under the pretext of driver's license databases, rather than the furor over a federal law. Spokesman: "The whole issue comes down to improving public safety, protecting national security and preventing identity fraud." ID cards do none of this.
