Rotten connected to Tri-State crematory story
Atlanta Journal-Constitution | Submitted by: Macki
Police found pictures of dead bodies on a computer owned by the family that ran the Tri-State Crematory before it was shut down by authorities. Those pictures do not portray bodies actually handled (or rather, not handled) by the Crematorium but instead were likely downloaded from Rotten.com's website. "It's not only possible, but there's a high probability of a student having viewed the particular site [Rotten.com]. We have a highly sophisticated filtering mechanism here at school, but this particular site doesn't fit the parameters of any filtering device, such as pornography." This is a strange thing to say, since Rotten is hardcoded by practically every filtering service on the planet.
